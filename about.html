About me

Hi, I'm Ryan

Rock Climber & Interior Designer

My name is Ryan Ta. I am 3rd year standing Interior Design student at Vancouver Island University. Born in Vietnam and raised in Canada, I have had the opportunity to study and work in different cultures and environments. I fell in love with rock climbing when a cousin introduced me to a nearby climbing gym. I climbed non-stop initially because I thought practicing every day would make me more robust and I would climb more challenging climbs. It was my mistake when I was inexperienced and without a leader. Therefore, I created this website as an online library of valuable articles to help beginners quickly learn and practice scientifically and avoid injury.
